Visit Singapore

A vibriant city state with rich culture, food and a shopping paradise.

Top three activities to do in Singapore

Jewel @ Singapore Changi Airport

First stop in Singapore, explore Jewel at Changi Airport. Immerse yourself in the world's tallest indoor waterfall, the Rain Vortex that is surrounded by a terraced forest setting. It is also a one stop shopping haven for luxury brands and street fashion Nike Flagship Store.

Nasi Lemak @ Tiong Bahru Food Centre

This food centre was established in 1951 houses of different dishes. From Local Toast served with eggs and Coffee to delicious Nasi Lemak

HydroDash at Sentosa

Go for sunbathing at Sentosa and play some watersport at HydroDash, a family-friendly inflatable water park. Challenge your family and friends to stay dry while navigating on the slippery obstacle course.
